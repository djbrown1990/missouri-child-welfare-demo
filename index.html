Executive Briefing

Missouri Child Welfare — Reconciliation & Compliance Gate

Grant reporting and compliance acceleration

Walkthrough Focus

  • Replace fragmented manual reporting with a governed, auditable flow.
  • Demonstrate mock-to-live path: Salesforce operations to AWS evidence.
  • Show fail-safe behavior before any downstream export is initiated.

Problem: Why teams lose reporting time

Current-state operations are accurate only after heavy manual cleanup.

  • Case data is spread across multiple systems with inconsistent IDs and formats.
  • Manual reconciliation introduces delays, rework, and avoidable compliance risk.
  • Grant reporting deadlines depend on tribal knowledge instead of deterministic controls.
  • Audit trails are incomplete when provenance and decisions are not captured in-line.

Use Case: Streamline grant reporting

Goal: move from spreadsheet governance to operational compliance by design.

Operational Intent

  • Reconcile messy records before export eligibility is evaluated.
  • Block non-compliant payloads with immediate remediation actions.
  • Publish only validated packages to AWS evidence targets.

Success Criteria

  • Repeatable checks, explicit ownership, and timestamped decisions.
  • Caseworker-visible tasking for unresolved data quality issues.
  • Audit-ready output with immutable package and run context.

Why current-state fails

The same household can appear differently across Salesforce pages and form sources.

Household / Account Contact Role Case Form Submission External Assertions
  • Address, county, and composition fields drift between household and submitted forms.
  • Role collisions occur when a contact is assigned conflicting workforce/caregiver roles.
  • Case dates and consent flags diverge across intake, external systems, and updates.
  • Missing state IDs and stale placements break downstream eligibility checks.

Reconciliation engine design

Deterministic controls first, heuristic confidence second.

Deterministic Layer

  • Required field and format checks (dates, phone normalization).
  • Exact key matches when IDs are present.
  • Source-priority rules from mapping configuration.

Heuristic Layer

  • Fuzzy name and address similarity scoring.
  • DOB proximity and role plausibility weighting.
  • Human-readable explanation per merge recommendation.
Conflict detection includes duplicate contacts across households, overlapping case windows, and contradictory guardian assignments.

Compliance gate workflow

No export leaves the system without passing policy checks.

  • Rules: required fields by stage, consent flags, PII restrictions, date logic, case eligibility.
  • Fail-fast mode blocks exports and creates remediation tasks for case managers.
  • Passing path writes audit metadata and prepares package + manifest.
  • All decisions are timestamped to support grant and audit review.
